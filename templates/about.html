About FitAura

FitAura is your personal health and fitness companion. It was built with the goal of helping users develop a healthier lifestyle using smart features like:

  • 📏 BMI calculator to track your fitness level
  • 😊The Mood Tracker feature lets users select how they feel from emojis like 😊, 😔, 😩, and more. Based on their mood, it displays uplifting, personalized quotes. It also includes task logging and water intake tracking to support daily wellness and emotional balance.
  • 🥗 Personalized diet plans (veg and non-veg) based on health condition
  • ⏱️ plan and do workout or exercises to reach your healthy and fitness Goal and Pomodoro-based fitness timer
  • 🤖 “AuraMate” chatbot for health tips and support

A comprehensive wellness platform built to help users track, manage, and improve their physical and mental health through an intuitive digital experience.
